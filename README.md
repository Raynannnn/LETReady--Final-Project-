# LETReady

**LETReady** is a web-based platform designed to provide CIE ICT students with organized, updated, and accessible LET reviewer materials. The platform addresses the lack of reliable resources and aims to make exam preparation more efficient and structured.

## Introduction

LETReady is created to help students efficiently prepare for the LET exam by consolidating resources in one accessible online platform. It ensures that reviewers are current, organized, and easy to navigate.

## Problem Statement

* Students currently have limited access to relevant and updated LET reviewers.
* Existing reviewers are often outdated, too broad, or expensive.
* Most materials are either printed or scattered online, making them hard to access.

## Features

* Centralized repository of LET reviewers.
* Updated and organized study materials.
* User-friendly interface for easy navigation.
